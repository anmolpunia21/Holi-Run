INSTRUCTIONS

  • There are three refreshment booths on the route. There will be color on each booth. You can take color from the booth and play.
  • Our volunteers may also throw color in air near refreshment point.
  • If you do not want to play with colors, let our volunteers know clearly. Also, run on the right side as much as possible.
  • Do not color anyone forcefully. If we receive any complaint or observe any indecent behaviour during run, there will be severe punishment.
  • There are no winners in the race. We recommend you to enjoy the route as much as possible by jogging and walking.
